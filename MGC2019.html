Midwest Geometry Conference 2019

September 6-8, 2019

Department of Mathematics
Iowa State University
Ames, Iowa (USA)




The conference topics are differential geometry and geometric analysis.
Graduate students, early career researchers and postdocs are strongly encouraged to attend.

The conference will begin on Friday at 1pm in Lagomarcino Hall (room W0272) and continue on Saturday and Sunday in Marston Hall (room 2155), at Iowa State University campus in Ames.

You can download the conference booklet and poster.
